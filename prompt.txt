- Attach supporting documents
- Leave reason / note

Leave Status Tracking
- Pending
- Approved
- Rejected
- Cancelled

Leave History
- View all past leave requests
- Filter by date, type, status

Leave Balance
- Remaining leave days
- Used leave
- Upcoming leave

Leave Calendar
- Personal leave calendar

Admin features:

Employee → Team Lead → HR → Final Approval

Features

- Expiry calculation

Annual Leave = 18 days / year
